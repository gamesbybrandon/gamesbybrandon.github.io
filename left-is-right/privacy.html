Left is Right — Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 20, 2025

Overview

Left is Right does not require you to create an account and we do not collect information that directly identifies you (such as your name or email) from inside the app.

What We Collect

  • No direct personal data. We don’t ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • Basic app/usage data. The game may record non-identifying events (e.g., settings, progress) locally on your device to make the game work properly.

Advertising (Third Parties)

If we show ads (e.g., via Google AdMob), the ad provider may collect device identifiers, coarse location, and usage data to show and measure ads. Their use is governed by their own privacy policy.

  • Google AdMob: Privacy Policy

Children’s Privacy

The game is intended for a general audience and not directed to children under 13. If you believe we have received information from a child, contact us and we will delete it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share non-identifying information with service providers strictly to operate the app (e.g., ad networks).

Your Choices

  • You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local data stored on your device.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app changes. We’ll post updates on this page and revise the “Effective date.”
